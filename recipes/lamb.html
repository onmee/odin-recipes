Marinated lamb chops

Ingredients

  1. 8 meaty lamb cutlets

    2. For the first marinade

  2. 15g garlic and ginger paste
  3. 1 tsp malt vinegar (or another vinegar)
  4. 1/3 tsp fine sea salt

    5. For the second marinade

  5. 2.5 tsp cumin powder
  6. 1 tsp coriander powder
  7. 1 clove
  8. 25g coriander
  9. 1.5 tsp garam masala
  10. 50g garlic and ginger paste
  11. 1 tsp black pepper
  12. 125g Greek style yoghurt (add more yoghurt if you want a creamier marinade)
  13. 35ml lime juice
  14. 10g red and green chillies (halve this if you want it to be less spicy)
  15. 1 tsp chilli powder
  16. 5g fine sea salt
  17. 2 tsp vegetable/olive oil
  18. Oil or butter to cook

Preparation & Cooking

  1. Place the lamb chops in a shallow dish. For the first marinade, mix all ingredients in a small bowl.
    Rub the marinade into the chops, cover and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  2. Blitz all the ingredients for the second marinade to a paste using a blender or food processor.
  3. After the 2 hours, rub the second marinade over the chops. Ensure that they are all properly coated.
    Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours (or until the next day).
  4. Remove the chops 2 hours before cooking, to allow them to warm up to room temperature.
  5. Add some olive oil or butter and heat a deep-frying pan to until very hot (until the oil smokes just a little),
    and then reduce the heat/flame to medium high.
  6. Cook chops for 6-8 minutes (user longer time if the chop is thicker or larger) whilst turning it over every minute.
  7. Allow it to rest for 2-3 minutes before serving.

Enjoy!
